HollyHood Haley J Missed WWE Tryout Due to Not Being Cleared for Epilepsy

WWE is the dream destination for many pro wrestlers out there. That being said, it just doesn’t work out for everyone, even if they get a tryout with the company.

HollyHood Haley J was supposed to be at WWE tryout, but that didn’t work out. Despite the setback, she is still determined to get match with Charlotte Flair.

Sean Sapp provided a bit more clarity behind Fightful’s paywall about HollyHood Haley J’s situation. It seems that she had a bit of an issue getting medical clearance from her doctor for the tryout.

“WWE sources confirmed that Hollyhood Haley J didn’t attend the tryouts due to clearances on her side, which she claims was her doctor not clearing her due to epilepsy. WWE sources told us they plan to have her back in for another tryout.”

HollyHood Haley J certainly brought a lot of attention to herself thanks to Netflix’s Wrestlers documentary. She brought a lot to the screen, and a lot of fans are also pulling for her right now.

Only time will tell if HollyHood Haley J will make her way to a WWE tryout. Her doctor apparently stood in her way this time around, but hopefully, that won’t be an issue in the future.
