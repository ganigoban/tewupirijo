Who Is Emma Stone Dating? The Man Who Stole Her Heart Rallshe

Emma Stone stands as one of Hollywood’s most beloved and talented actresses, boasting an impressive portfolio of roles in films like La La Land, The Favourite, and Cruella.

But what about her personal life? Who is the man fortunate enough to capture her heart and become her husband and the father of her baby girl? Here’s everything you need to know about Dave McCary, Emma Stone’s partner in love and laughter.

He’s a Director on Saturday Night Live

Dave McCary serves as a writer, director, and producer for the renowned comedy show Saturday Night Live. Joining the team in 2013 as a segment director, he has contributed to some of the show’s most uproarious and unforgettable sketches. McCary is also a co-founder of the comedy group Good Neighbor, alongside his SNL colleagues Kyle Mooney, Beck Bennett, and Nick Rutherford.

Their Meeting on SNL in 2016

The couple’s romantic journey began on the set of SNL when Emma Stone hosted the show in December 2016. She starred in a sketch titled “Wells for Boys,” directed by McCary.

The sketch, a parody of a Fisher-Price commercial featuring a sensitive boy playing with a well, struck a chord with critics and fans alike, sparking a connection between Stone and McCary.

Proposal at the SNL Offices in 2019

After over two years of dating, McCary proposed to Stone in a deeply romantic and meaningful setting – the SNL offices where their love story began. The proposal, described as private and intimate, unfolded without any onlookers. McCary shared the joyful news on his Instagram, posting a photo of the couple beaming with happiness, showcasing Stone’s pearl engagement ring.

Marriage in 2020

Amidst the backdrop of the coronavirus pandemic, the couple exchanged vows in a discreet ceremony in September 2020 in Los Angeles. Sporting matching wedding bands, they solidified their union with only a select few close friends and family members in attendance.

Welcoming Their Baby Girl in 2021

On March 13, 2021, Stone and McCary welcomed their first child, a baby girl named Louise Jean McCary, paying homage to Stone’s grandmother. Although Stone never publicly confirmed her pregnancy, she was spotted with a baby bump in January 2021. Sources revealed their overwhelming joy and love for their daughter, as they embraced the journey of parenthood.

Supportive of Each Other’s Careers

The foundation of Stone and McCary’s relationship rests on mutual respect and support for each other’s professional endeavors. McCary takes pride in Stone’s accomplishments and frequently accompanies her to award shows and premieres. In turn, Stone admires McCary’s talent and creativity, praising his work on SNL and his directorial debut, Brigsby Bear.

Valuing Privacy and Intimacy

Unlike many celebrity couples, Stone and McCary prefer to maintain a low profile, shielding their relationship from public scrutiny. They refrain from sharing intimate details on social media or granting interviews about their personal lives. Preferring quiet nights at home or intimate gatherings with friends over glitzy events, they cherish their privacy and the intimacy of their relationship.

Conclusion

Emma Stone and Dave McCary epitomize love and authenticity in Hollywood. Their enduring marriage, beautiful daughter, and successful careers stand as a testament to their bond.

Beginning their journey on SNL, they continue to bring laughter and joy into each other’s lives, proving that simplicity and sweetness often form the strongest relationships.
