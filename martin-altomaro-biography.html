Martin Altomaro Biography

BIOGRAPHY
Martin Altomaro is an actor born on May 26, 1976, in Neuquén Province, Argentina, although he moved with his family to the city of Cuernavaca, in Morelos, Mexico, when he was 5 years old. Of both nationalities, Martin is a renowned theater and television actor, who has remained active since his debut in 1992, with the film Anoche soñé tú , by Marisa Sistach, alongside Leticia Perdigón, José Alonso and Socorro Bonilla.

Martín became well known among the Mexican public, after a long participation in a series of commercials for the Nissan brand, which told a story of a courtship over several years. In his long career, with more than 40 credits, Martín has participated in films such as Ámbar (1994), Libre de culpas (1997), Demasiado amor (2001), Así del precipicio (2006), Aqui entre nos (2011), Tercera Call (2013), Doctor Funes’ Formula (2015) and, recently, Todo Mal (2018) by Issa López.

On television, the actor has participated in Nada personal (1996), La Doubt (2002), Como ama una mujer (2007) and Soy tu fan (2010-2012), in the role of Nico Cruz, perhaps the most popular character and loved by everyone he has played.
