Selena Gomez Has a &quot;Secret Project&quot; Coming Up

Selena Gomez has a lot lined up for 2017, like recording new music and collaborating with Coach (and possibly pursuing a new romance with The Weeknd). But according to new photos on Instagram, the pop star has a "secret project" underway as well.

Makeup artist Hung Vanggo Instagrammed a selfie of the star in Los Angeles—all made up and sporting a freshly-cropped lob—with the hashtags #bts (behind the scenes) and #secretproject.

Vanggo also tagged hairstylist Marissa Marino, celebrity manicurist Tom Bachik and stylist Chris Classen, who all Instagrammed the same picture with the #secretproject tag. Classen added "shhhhh....." in his caption.

It seems the Gomez pic is from a photoshoot, but it's still unclear what it could be for. Yesterday, photographer Mert Alas posted a behind-the-scenes picture of the star stripped down nearly nude, which he has since deleted. However, it seems this was from a separate shoot, considering the wardrobe (or lack thereof) and massive hoop earrings.

Mert Alas / Instagram

Regardless of what the shoots are for, they just prove that Selena Gomez is coming back in a major way this year.
