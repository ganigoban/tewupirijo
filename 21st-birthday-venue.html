10 Best 21st Birthday Party Venues For Rent in Las Vegas, NV

From sports to hiking, Vegas has what you're looking for. Not to mention venues suitable for the occasion. If you want to have your 21st birthday party in a more exclusive venue, consider renting a party hall, event center room, or restaurant dining space. You can have the venue handle the decorating and then include activities like wine tasting classes or a DJ for dancing. The venue will also have options for catering if you don't want to bother with menus.
If you want to leave the planning to someone else you can book party packages with Vegas Party VIP and other brands. They offer comprehensive plans for a 21st birthday that include limo service, nightclub access, and food/drink packages at some of the most exclusive dining establishments in town.
Las Vegas resort hotels are popular choices because they have hundreds of things to do! Hotels like the MGM Grand and Bellagio offer live entertainment, fine dining, mini golf, laser tag, swimming pools, spa treatments, art exhibitions, and hiking day trips. Just to name a few possibilities for your 21st birthday party. You can rest assured that when you choose a hotel on the Strip you'll find endless things to do without ever leaving the resort!
