Aldi's Wooden Ladder Shelf Displays All Your Favorite Plants

Spring is here, and that means we’re going more gaga over our plants than usual, just because we’re so excited (and in some cases, desperate) for the world to warm up a little bit and for things to turn green. Our houseplant frenzy coincides with our impulses to spring clean and reorganize, and luckily, Aldi is right there with us, as their weekly Aldi Finds show. From area rugs to storage containers, it seems like the store is totally on the same page with us when it comes to giving the house a seasonal refresh, but it’s their Belavi Wooden Plant Ladder Stand that’s really caught our eyes.

Belavi Wooden Plant Ladder Stand

This item must be popular at Aldi, because this isn’t the first time it’s been an Aldi Find. However, this year it’s much cheaper, selling for just $24.99 compared to last year’s $39.99 price tag. The wooden ladder plant stands are 39 inches tall and 43 inches wide at the bottom, and they can hold quite a bit of weight, too, which is great if you have some heavy potted plants you want to display.

Our other favorite thing about a stand like this is that you can spread your plants out, so they all get enough sunlight. And according to blogger Aldi Reviewer, these ladder plant stands are easy to assemble and sturdy — they got the stand at Aldi in 2021, and it’s still holding up.

What if there’s not an Aldi nearby? We found a similar plant stand on Amazon. It’s a little bit more expensive, but not by much, and still offers three shelves for plant display. It also comes in three different colors, and has great reviews: 4.6 out of 5 stars from 221 ratings.

If you’re ready to surround yourself with plants this spring, pick up an affordable ladder-style plant stand from Aldi or Amazon today. You can get your seedlings ready for outdoor planting, or finally give your houseplants the spot in the sun they deserve.
