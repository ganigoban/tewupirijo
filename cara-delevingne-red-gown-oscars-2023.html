Cara Delevingne Looks Regal in a Red Gown at the 2023 Oscars

Cara Delevingne knows how to make an entrance.

The actress and model arrived at the 2023 Oscars tonight in a timeless red gown pulled straight from Elie Saab's fall/winter 2021 couture collection. The classic off-the shoulder piece made her look like an Old Hollywood princess and featured a giant bow on one shoulder, a high leg slit, and a dramatic skirt that flowed onto the champagne-colored carpet.

Delevingne finished the look with a pair of shiny red satin platform sandals with straps at the ankles by Stuart Weitzman, and strings of Bulgari diamonds.

Arturo Holmes//Getty Images

Her jewelry included a three-layer diamond necklace, a matching twist-around bracelet, a massive diamond ring, and diamond cluster earrings.

She kept her glam equally sophisticated, opting for rosy eye shadow and blush, a pink lip, a soft pink manicure, and a slicked-back bun.

Prior to the ceremony, she prepped her skin with treatments from Joanna Czech Skincare and Lyma Laser.

Delevingne's attendance at the 95th annual Academy Awards comes after she opened up in a candid interview published last week about overcoming her recent addiction battle. Among other things, she admitted to hitting rock bottom in terms of substance abuse and depression, and said her friends and rehab saved her life. She has since committed to sobriety.

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

Now healthy and back to work, the star made a stunning red-carpet appearance last month at the 2023 Screen Actors Guild Awards, where she wore a gorgeous black gown by Carolina Herrera paired with De Beers diamond jewelry. For that occasion, she also went with a classic makeup look: bronzy eye shadow and a red lip.
