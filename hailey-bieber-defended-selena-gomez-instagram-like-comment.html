How Hailey Bieber Defended Selena Gomez on Instagram

Selena Gomez and Hailey Bieber have moved on from their drama, and they would like all of their fans to move on, too.

Last night, Bieber posted a statement on her Instagram Story imploring her followers not to post any “mean or rude comments on my behalf on anyone else’s posts.” Her post comes after Gomez was body-shamed over a recent Instagram photo.

In an additional show of peace, Bieber liked the post on which Gomez was receiving hateful comments. In it, Gomez smiles at the camera, wearing a Dior sweater and cream tulle skirt.

Bieber asked her fans to stop harassing people once and for all, in her full statement. She wrote:

If you’re leaving mean or rude comments on my behalf on anyone’s posts just know that I don’t want that, nor do I ever or will I ever support or condone hateful, mean, or nasty comments. Doing that is not supporting me..

If you are participating in that, you are a part of a culture that I want no part of.

Please be nice or don’t say anything

Hailey Bieber’s like on Selena Gomez’s Instagram

Gomez, for her part, has ignored the drama; instead, she shared today that she is in Paris and working on new music. “Don’t worry guys, it’s coming. Even from Paris. 😉,” she wrote.

Gomez has also defended Bieber before, though.

She posted a similar message directed at her own fans in the spring, when Bieber was receiving hate on TikTok over her history with Gomez. The Rare Beauty founder wrote, “Hailey Bieber reached out to me and let me know that she has been receiving death threats and such hateful negativity. This isn’t what I stand for. No one should have to experience hate or bullying. I’ve always advocated for kindness and really want this all to stop. ♥️”
