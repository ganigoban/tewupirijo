Kristen Stewart Wore the No Pants Trend With a High-Cut Bodysuit

The pantless trend continues to reign supreme, with Kristen Stewart being the latest Hollywood starlet to try it. On March 5, the actress hit the red carpet of her latest movie, Love Lies Bleeding, and stepped out in one of her most daring looks to date—a high-cut, black leotard and no pants.

Stewart's leotard, a piece designed by lingerie brand Cosabella, featured a square neckline with thin straps with the base of the bodysuit descending into a deep V. The 33-year-old completed the look with a black Bettter blazer, sheer black Wolford tights, black Chanel pumps, and multiple silver rings as accessories.

The bold look follows Stewart's latest Rolling Stone cover, in which she went braless in a black leather vest and wore high-cut white underwear that she boldly stuck her hands in. Elsewhere during the shoot, Stewart showed more skin, wearing a loose red Nike tank with another pair of white undies.

Speaking about her recent clothing choice for the magazine and the right-wing backlash it received, the Twilight actress revealed that the pictures were meant to appeal to her younger self. "I just felt like it was fun," Stewart told Entertainment Tonight on the red carpet. "I just wanted to make pictures that I would have taped to my ceiling as a teenager."

Stewart is the latest of a string of celebrities to give the pantless trend a try, following in the footsteps of Hailey Bieber, Emma Corrin, and more.
