Dua Lipas Latest Instagram Photo Dump Is a Fashion Dream

Dua Lipa’s newest post on Instagram is filled to the brim with fashion.

Today, the international pop star shared a series of photos that documented a recent trip to France, including several jaw-dropping outfits.

In one mirror selfie, the “Don’t Start Now” singer poses in her hotel bed while clad in a sultry red-hot lingerie set composed of a lacy cami and a wrap skirt. In another picture, Lipa looks sleek and modern in a white Prada maxi dress with a crisp collar, buttons all the way down the center, and a small train. She accessorized simply by carrying a black Celine clutch.

In the next selfie, she wears stacks of gold Veneda Carter chain necklaces with a white ribbed tank top and a lime-green leather jacket.

Lipa also shared a car selfie in which she wore a bright blue top and matching neck scarf underneath a black-and-white leather biker jacket from Gucci. She kicked it up a notch by wearing even more Veneda Carter jewelry, including chunky gemstone-encrusted gold rings, plus Shay Diamond pavé hoop earrings.

“🇫🇷💋,” she wrote in the caption.

The Grammy winner’s recent trip to the French Riviera marked plenty of new milestones.

First, she made her red-carpet debut at the Cannes Film Festival while wearing a head-turning black cutout gown by Celine. Her boyfriend, Romain Gavras, accompanied her to the star-studded affair.

Later that same week, Lipa joined Donatella Versace at a waterfront estate in Cannes to debut a co-designed Versace collection called “La Vacanza.”
