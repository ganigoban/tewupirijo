Josh Peck children: Meet Max Milo Peck

Joshua Michael Peck is an American Actor, Comedian and a YouTuber.

Peck was born on November 10, 1986, and began his career in acting even as a child.

In the early 1990s and 2000s, Peck was cast on the Amanda Show which aired from 2000 to 2002.

His rise to fame was the Josh Nicholas alongside Drake Bell’s Character in the Nickelodeon sitcom Drake and Josh from 2004 and 2007.

Other movies he has been cast include Mean Creek (2004), Drillbit Taylor (2008), The Wackness (2008), ATM (2012), Red Dawn (2012), Battle of the Year (2013), Danny Collins (2015), and Take the 10 (2017) and played the main character role in the Disney+ original series Turner & Hooch, a continuation of the 1989 movie Turner & Hooch.

Josh Peck Children: Meet Max Milo Peck

Josh Peck married the love of his life in 2017. His wife is called Paige O’Brien. They have a son together called Max Milo Peck. Their son was born in December 2018.
