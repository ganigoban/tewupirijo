Coffin Match Added To AEW All In London

AEW All In London is going to be quite a show. The main event and the kickoff show might have some of the same people as well, but the show will also be unique, because AEW is bringing back the coffin match.

Click here for our complete coverage of AEW Dynamite this week.

Sting made big return this week on AEW Dynamite. That busted up Swerve Strickland’s big plan for the Mogul Embassy to take over. Now, a big match is booked for All In London with a rather morbid stipulation.

AEW has put on a couple coffin matches in the past. This time around, they will hold a tag team encounter where the losers will find themselves in a grim location. During AEW Dynamite this week, it was confirmed that Tony Khan booked Sting and Darby Allin vs AR Fox and Swerve Strickland in a coffin match.

Wembley Stadium will be packed to the brim, full of excited UK pro wrestling fans. Now, they will have one more epic match to look forward to. We will have full coverage of the AEW All In London event. Keep checking back with Ringside News for more!
