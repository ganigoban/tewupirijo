Im a Dollar Tree super fan my fave perfume dupes for $1.25 from Burberry to Gucci

DESIGNER buys don't have to come at a designer price point when it comes to perfumes.

There are dupes on the market for some of your favorite pricey perfume brands, and Dollar Tree has you covered.

A woman named Kimberly has discovered several cheap dupes for high-end perfumes that usually cost a pretty penny.

Some TikTok users have said they're perfect to mist around your car for an amazing smelling interior.

"Do you guys know that Dollar Tree has tons of dupe perfumes for higher-end fragrances for only a dollar?" Kimberly began in a video posted to her 37K TikTok followers.

The first perfume, My EAD, is the perfect dupe for the floral scent My Burberry by Burberry which retails for $133.
