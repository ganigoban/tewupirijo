Here's How Much Kelly Dodd Is Really Worth

There's been no word from the network or the Real Housewives of Orange County production company, Evolution Media, regarding whether Kelly Dodd will have a future on the show after Season 15. Not all Bravo shows share the same production company, but Evolution is also behind Vanderpump Rules, so they might make a similar call with Kelly Dodd as they did with the four cast members they fired from Pump Rules. (However, RHOC is reportedly in a state of disarray, so keeping old cast members might take precedence right now.) 

That all being said, Kelly will probably be OK if they do get rid of her. She was reportedly making $150,000 in Season 11, when she signed on as the lowest-paid cast member, according to Radar Online, although that number has likely gone up. Vicki Gunvalson was reportedly making $420,000 per season by the time she left the show, a drop from her previous paycheck of $1.26 million, per Radar Online.

Overall, Kelly is reportedly worth $10 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. Not too shabby, right?

Kelly's net worth might increase if she ends up marrying her fiance, Fox News channel correspondent Rick Leventhal. TBD.
