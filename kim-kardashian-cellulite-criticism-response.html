Kim Kardashian Claps Back at Cellulite Critics

Kim Kardashian set the internet abuzz (again) after her cellulite was visible when she wore a bikini on vacation. (God forbid!) Though some fans celebrated that she showed off her real body, others were quick to fat-shame her. But the reality star didn't let her critics go unnoticed. Last night, she went off on a Twitter rant (which she has since deleted) telling off her haters.

“Yup I’ve recently seen perspective is a bitch. I’ll work on taking good videos with better lighting &angles. Fuck you," she snarkily tweeted, according to The Sun.

Splash News

Kardashian reportedly shared the post at around 11:22 p.m. at her local time in Mexico, where she's vacationing at the moment, but removed it a minute later. However, that didn't stop a handful of news sites from spotting and reporting on her statement.

We don't know if her comment was in response to Piers Morgan's sexist comments ("Flaws should not be celebrated."), but it might as well have been.
