Barry Corbin Net Worth

Barry Corbin is an American actor born on October 16, 1940 in Lamesa, TX. He is best known for his role as Whitey, the supportive coach on the CW drama One Tree Hill, alongside Chad Michael Murray. He also received an Emmy nomination for his work in the television series Northern Exposure.
