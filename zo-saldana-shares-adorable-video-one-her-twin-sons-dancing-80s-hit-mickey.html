Watch Zo Saldana's Twin Son Shake His Tiny Booty to &quot;Mickey&quot;

Prepare yourself for some serious next-level cuteness. Actress Zoë Saldana shared a video on Instagram of one of her 1-year-old twin sons rocking out to the epic '80s hit "Mickey" by Toni Basil. And if we had to sum it in one word: amazing.

"'Oh Mickey!' #twins #lovethe80s #dance #be #free," Saldana captioned the clip (below) of the little guy rocking back and forth to the catchy one-hit wonder and wiggling his diapered booty. And he's already got some impressive rhythm! Watch as he starts banging his head along with the drums at the end:

The Guardians of the Galaxy star and her husband Marco Perego welcomed twins Bowie Ezio Perego-Saldana and Cy Aridio Perego-Saldana in November 2014. At the L.A. launch celebration of the go90 app in September, Saldana told InStyle that the most surprising thing about having children is "how fun it is."

"I guess I was surrounded by way too many moms who are always like, 'Oh, I got it so hard,'" she said. "And my sister was always like, 'It's a piece of cake. It's super awesome.' So I stuck to her, and it was fun."
