Michelle McCool Was Not Asked To Attend RAW XXX

Michelle McCool emerged as one of the strongest and most competitive female WWE superstars of all time. The Flawless Diva was featured in high-profile programs and received massive opportunities at a time when women in WWE were not taken seriously. However, McCool recently revealed that she won’t be a part of the upcoming RAW XXX episode.

The longest-running weekly episodic show in television history is set to celebrate its 30th anniversary. The auspicious occasion will be commemorated with a special edition coming up this Monday.

RAW XXX will feature an exclusive line-up of superstars, both from past and present. Some of the legends announced for the show include Shawn Michaels, Hulk Hogan, Ric Flair, and The Undertaker.

However, it seems like communication is not looking good between WWE and The Undertaker’s wife, Michelle McCool. The former WWE Divas champion recently revealed that she was not approached to be part of the upcoming Royal Rumble event.

Michelle McCool once again took to Twitter to reply to a fan that she was not invited to attend RAW XXX either. However, she did show excitement about her husband, The Undertaker, appearing on the show.

“Didn’t get the raw call either but my hubs is there.” 

WWE also didn’t call Michelle McCool for the Women’s Royal Rumble match this year. She had to break that news previously on Twitter that the company never asked her to show up.

Michelle McCool has paved the way for the younger generation to get opportunities that she and the superstars of her era did not get. Moreover, it is even surprising that McCool was not approached while The Undertaker was, indicating that a rift might have been created between both parties.
