Shoppers Say They Feel Sexy and Confident in These Stretchy $9 Leggings

Depending on where you live, you may be struggling to get dressed these days. It’s no longer freezing outside, but balmy spring weather hasn’t quite bloomed yet. During transitional fashion moments like this, I turn back to wardrobe basics (think: leggings, lightweight sweaters, and white sneakers). And a quick Amazon search for new staples led me to the popular Walifrey leggings, which start at just $9 with an on-site coupon. 

Available in 24 colors and patterns, the pants are made from a “silky-smooth” material, per a reviewer. They come in both capri- and ankle-lengths with the option to add on side pockets. Plus, they have a flattering high waistband that a shopper confirmed “does not roll down,” even while bending over. 

These leggings go with pretty much everything in your closet; wear them casually around the house or to run errands with an oversized sweater and sneakers, dress them down for the gym with a workout tank and athletic shoes, or zhuzh them up for an evening out with a long cardigan and heeled booties. 

As for why the stretchy pants are so popular, you’ll find the answer in their Amazon reviews section. More than 1,000 people have purchased the leggings in the past month, per the retailer, and hundreds of those shoppers left rave reviews. One person who was “skeptical because the price was so reasonable” said that the “quality was great and they fit well.” Someone else confirmed that the “leggings are very comfortable” and “you cannot see through them at all.” 

Other shoppers commented on the way the leggings made them feel. One reviewer said the pants “offer smoothing control without suffocating you” and make them “feel sexy and confident.” Another reviewer called the pants “absolutely perfect” since they’re “curve-friendly and move with you, not against you.” And a third person who “only wears leggings for pants” said these “might be the best leggings [they’ve] ever bought” — now that’s a high praise. 

It’s not often that top-rated leggings are available for as low as $9, so I highly recommend you grab a Walifrey pair from Amazon before word gets out. Your future self will thank you.
