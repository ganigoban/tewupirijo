Top WWE NXT Talents Contract Set to Expire Soon

A high-profile NXT talent’s WWE contract is set to expire within the next few months.

According to Fightful Select, Dijak, formerly known as Dominik Dijakovic and T-Bar during his time in WWE, is scheduled to see his contract with the company conclude in June of 2024. This year marks a significant period for WWE contract renewals, as numerous talents inked five-year deals back in 2019, a pivotal moment coinciding with the emergence of AEW.

Dijak’s journey in WWE has had various personas and stints, including a stint on the main WWE roster as part of Retribution. However, his most notable and impactful tenure has been his current stint in NXT, where he has delivered several memorable and standout matches.

Before his WWE tenure, Dijak had an extensive wrestling background, having previously worked for Ring of Honor and extensively on the independent wrestling circuit.
