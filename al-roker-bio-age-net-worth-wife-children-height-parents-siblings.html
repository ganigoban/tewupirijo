Al Roker Bio, Age, Net Worth, Wife, Children, Height, Parents, Siblings

Al Roker, born Albert Lincoln Roker Jr. is a well-known American weather presenter, journalist, television personality, and author.

Al Roker is the current weather anchor on NBC’s Today, and occasionally co-hosts 3rd Hour Today.

Many know he has an inactive American Meteorological Society Television Seal #238.
