DaBoii Steals The Show In SOB x RBE's &quot;Onna Gang&quot; Video

Filmed on location in Vallejo, California, replete with aerial views and such, "Onna Gang" is everything you'd expect from a group that puts careful detail into everything they publish. The video single is in large part group's showcase of DaBoii, in the same vein Yhung T.O. puts himself under the microscope in "Misunderstood." The only difference is, this video is less personal, and more indicative of the "old soul" approach to life DaBoii seems to embody (within the group).

The video follows a day in the life on the block, DaBoii riding shotgun in a "old school." DaBoii's breathless delivery offsets the captivating visuals picked up the videographer, who takes his job of extracting the essential elements of Vallejo very seriously. Every image or shot tells a story in itself. DaBoii and even Lul G are the most forthright members of group, but they do their talking when it counts the most.
