Ricky Reyes teases plans to propose to new Russian girlfriend Will he return to Before the 90 Da

Remember Ricky Reyes from Season 2 of 90 Day Fiance: Before the 90 Days? He’s still looking for international love and he may have found it with a woman in Russia.

Ricky has been keeping his followers updated as he continues to work on his physique. When he was on Before the 90 Days, Ricky was a bit chubby, but now, he’s been putting in regular workouts in an effort to tighten up and get ready to woo his new girlfriend.

It looks like he’ll be heading to see her soon as well, and when he does, Ricky is teasing that he may even propose. On Thursday, he posted a picture of the new girlfriend and wrote in the caption, “There were a lot of up and downs with her, a lot more downs than up due to my crazy ass. Less than a week, we shall see how it goes …. maybe she’ll get something shiny.”

So far, it doesn’t look like cameras will be following Ricky abroad this time, despite all of his updates. It also looks like Ricky and this new woman almost didn’t make it but were able to make up after what Ricky described as “major differences.”

Ricky made his way into the 90 Day Fiance family back when he went to Colombia, looking for love with a woman named Melissa. Many TLC viewers will remember that, when Melissa didn’t work out, Ricky had a backup plan.

He quickly moved on to Ximena and tried to win her heart. However, when Ximena realized she was the second woman Ricky tried to connect with during his trip, she quickly pushed him to the side.

Will Ricky find forever love this time around? It sure looks like he’s going to try.
