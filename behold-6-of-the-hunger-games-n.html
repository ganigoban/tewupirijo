Behold 6 Of The Hunger Games Nail Polish Colors On My Nails

It seemed like you guys were really feeling The Hunger Games nail polish collection when we posted about it back in December so here's a closer look at the actual colors in the China Glaze range many of which, I'm happy to report, are even prettier in person. Come check 'em out.

Okay, pardon the messiness here! On the left we have Fast Track, Riveting, Luxe and Lush (over my current mani - a vanilla shade), Stone Cold and Electrify and on the right, my favorite color in the collection, Foie Gras.

Here they are in the bottles (atop our very new, very special March issue).

The colors hit stores, including Ulta and Sally Beauty, on March 1st and will retail for $7 each. Meanwhile, the film comes out on March 23rd!
