Rick Hendrick Net Worth

Rick Hendrick was born on July 12, 1949 in Warrentown, NC. He is the founder of the Hendrick Automotive Group, one of the largest automotive retail organizations in the United States. He is also the owner of Hendrick Motorsports, a NASCAR team that has won a record 11 NASCAR Cup Series owner championships. Hendrick is a successful businessman and philanthropist, and his success has made him one of the most influential figures in the world of motorsports.
