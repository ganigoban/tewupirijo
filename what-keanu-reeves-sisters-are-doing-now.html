What Keanu Reeves' Sisters Are Doing Now

Not much is known about Emma Rose Reeves, Keanu's half-sister from his father, Samuel Reeves. Staying away from the spotlight, she doesn't have much of a relationship with her famous brother. Speaking to The Mirror (via Whoa Is (Not) Me) in 2003, Emma Rose explained, "I would love to have a relationship with Keanu," adding, "I don't want anything from him — not money, not fame by association — just to get to know my big brother. I see all his films and read all the articles about him, but it's not the same as being part of his life."

Keanu's relationship on his dad's side has always been strained. In 2001, Samuel spoke to the Honolulu Star-Bulletin, explaining how his substance abuse created a rift between himself and his A-list son. "He's made it clear that he wants nothing to do with me. My feeling was so intense that it told me how much I missed the kid. It's been so long for everything."

Samuel Reeves passed away in 2018, but it seems like that hasn't changed much in terms of Keanu's relationship with his half-sister. According to The Victor O'Hana Facebook page (via Mr.-Reeves.com), Emma Rose hosted a memorial luncheon in light of Samuel Reeves' passing, but none of her father's other children were in attendance.

If you were shocked to learn that Reeves has multiple siblings, you'll never believe what else you don't know about the actor's life.
