Kyle Kuzma Allegedly Tried To Slide In Farrah Abraham's DM's

Kyle Kuzma has been one of the few bright spots on the Los Angeles Lakers this season and has been taking full advantage of the Los Angeles lifestyle. As a 23-year-old man, it's probably a great time to be in L.A. right now. Although, what hasn't been so great for Kuzma is his attempts at winning the affection of Farrah Abraham who of course rose to prominence thanks to her time on the show Teen Mom.

Abraham recently appeared on the Hollywood Pipeline podcast where she talked about how Kuzma tried to slide into her dm's. She eventually asked him to give her courtside tickets to a Lakers game, but as Farrah described, Kuzma said he'd have to drop $15k out of pocket and refused to do it. That's when she kindly told him to go away.

Kuzma probably doesn't care too much because recently he's been seen chilling on boats with Instagram model Katya Elise Henry. Henry has also been seen at a few Lakers games as of late so we're sure he's moved on.

As for basketball though, well, the Lakers are in trouble. The team is in 11th place in the Western Conference with a record of 29-31 and are struggling to string consecutive wins together in order to go on a playoff run.
