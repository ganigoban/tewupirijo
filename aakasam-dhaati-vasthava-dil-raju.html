Aakasam Dhaati Vasthava.. Dil Raju!

Dil Raju is one of the top producers in the country and is known to give chances to debutante directors and actors in Telugu cinema. His previous film, Balagam, which introduced actor Venu Yeldandi as a director has become a huge hit at the box office.

Well, now Dil Raju is all set to introduce one more talented person as the main lead. Noted choreographer Yaswanth Master is getting introduced as the main lead in the upcoming project of Dilraju Productions.

Today makers announced officially with a short title glimpse. The film touted to be a musical and dance based love story, titled as “Aakasam Dhaati Vasthava.” Talentdd Malyalam actress Karthika Muralidaran, CIA fame will be playing the female lead in the film.

With this film, a debutante named Sasi Kumar Muthiluri will be turning director. What’s special is that the film’s music will be composed by a popular singer in Telugu cinema. So, this film is going to be special in every way. Harshith Reddy and Hansitha Reddy bankrolling the film and Sirish will be presenting.
