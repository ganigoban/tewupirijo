Scarlett Johansson - Biography

Scarlett is best-known for her performance in Black Widow

    Who is Scarlett Johansson?

    Scarlett Johansson became one of Hollywood's hottest stars at the age of just 19 with her performance in 2004's . Despite the vulnerable, complex characters she has become known for, Scarlett's rise was marked by a confidence beyond her years.

    As a child growing up in New York, she regularly auditioned for advertising work. But the youngster's distinctive features and husky voice didn't sit well with ad execs in search of the all-American kid. 

    Her Acting Career

    Despite numerous rebuttals, however, the youngster was determined not to give up; she planned on following in the footsteps of another child star, Jodie Foster all the way to the top. She enrolled at the Lee Strasberg Theatre Institute For Young People and within just a few months won a speaking part opposite Ethan Hawke in the Broadway play  

    But it was in the 1998 romance , as Kristin Scott Thomas' troubled daughter, that she caught Tinseltown's attention. The movie's leading man Robert Redford described her as "13 going on 30" and top casting agents immediately realised there was a hot new talent in town.

    Scarlett grew up in New York, the joint youngest of four children she has a twin brother, Hunter. Her father, an architect, immigrated from Denmark at the age of 27, leaving behind his first wife and Scarlett's half-brother. 

    In the Big Apple Karsten Johansson married again and established a living in New York's building trade. Those close to Scarlett talked of her having a wisdom beyond her years. 

    "She makes you feel like she has been around the world," said director Sofia Coppola. "She has a coolness and a subtlety that you would not expect. You feel like she's seen a lot." 

    Despite being very much a New Yorker, Scarlett took to the Hollywood scene with aplomb. The A-list status had its benefits, such as an art collection, a cool BMW and even cooler boyfriends. She's dated Josh Hartnett and Sean Penn. 

    Her Personal Life

    From 2008 to 2010, Scarlett was married to Ryan Reynolds. Her next serious relationship was with French creative agency boss Romain Dauriac. The couple announced their engagement in 2013 and Scarlett became pregnant soon after.

    Meanwhile, in 2020, the actress tied the knot with American comedian Colin Jost. Together they share a son called Cosmo. 

    Having proved herself as an actress she began to set sights on future challenges, which include testing her mettle behind the camera. Most first-time directors start off with a short. Scarlett, on the other hand, is more inclined to think big. "I'd rather just dive into a feature," she says. 

    "I don't know if I'd necessarily be good at making a short film. I definitely want to make a big epic film, not necessarily , but a larger than life subject I want to make all kinds of movies, I'm totally ambitious."
